ABOUT OADBY EVINGTON THURMASTON CONTACT

Thurmaston Karate Club was founded to provide high-quality traditional karate training for those looking to develop both physically and mentally. The club offers a disciplined yet supportive environment, making it an ideal place for students of all ages and abilities to train and grow.

With a strong focus on Wado Ryu Karate, instruction is led by experienced practitioners, Sensei John and Sensei Smith. Training is continually enhanced by learning from some of the most respected karate practitioners in the country, ensuring students receive well-rounded and high-level tuition.

Beyond physical technique, the club emphasizes the values of respect, perseverance, and self-discipline, helping students build confidence both inside and outside the dojo. Whether training for fitness, self-defense, or personal growth, every member is supported on their journey to reaching their full potential in karate.

How to Find Us

Thurmaston Karate Club is located at Thurmaston Memorial Hall on Melton Road, LE4 8BD.

Oadby Karate Club is the longest-running of our three clubs and has been a key part of the community for over fifteen years. Originally founded by Sensei Lee Goold, the club was later led by Sensei Russell Goold before transitioning to new leadership. Today, Sensei Steve Smith and Sensei John run the club together, continuing its strong tradition of Wado Ryu karate.

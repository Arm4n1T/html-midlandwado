ABOUT OADBY EVINGTON THURMASTON CONTACT

Oadby, Evington, and Thurmaston – three clubs, one tradition.

We are a close-knit community of karate enthusiasts, training in Oadby, Evington, and Thurmaston. Whether you're a beginner or experienced, we welcome you to learn Wado Ryu Karate in a supportive and disciplined environment!

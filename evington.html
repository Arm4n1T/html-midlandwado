Evington Karate Club was established in 2004 with the goal of providing high-quality traditional karate training to anyone with a genuine interest in developing both mentally and physically.

The club fosters a disciplined yet welcoming atmosphere, ensuring that training is accessible and beneficial for all ages and abilities.

With a strong foundation in Wado Ryu Karate, instruction is guided by experienced practitioners, including Sensei Smith and Sensei John. Training is continuously enriched by learning from some of the best karate practitioners in the country, maintaining a commitment to excellence and growth in the art.

How to Find Us

Evington Karate Club is located at Evington Leisure Centre on Downing Drive, LE5 6LP.
